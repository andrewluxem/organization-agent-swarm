# Workflow: Cross-Functional Launch

1. Worldwide orchestrator frames the outcome, constraints, and decision.
2. Affiliate lead evaluates partnerships, publishers, creators, and onsite distribution.
3. Automated Advertising lead designs channel, audience, creative, offer, and outbound execution.
4. Analytics lead defines knowledge, segmentation, forecast, and measurement requirements.
5. Specialists work in bounded, non-overlapping task packets.
6. Cross-Channel Measurement performs an independent acceptance review.
7. The orchestrator returns one recommendation.
8. A human approves or rejects release actions.
