# Governance

The swarm is built to **propose**, not to release. Its governance keeps work
bounded, evidence honest, and irreversible actions in human hands. These pages
summarize the governance concepts that live in the repository's `AGENTS.md`,
`CONTRIBUTING.md`, `SECURITY.md`, `DISCLAIMER.md`, `team/operating-model.md`, and
`team/handoff-contract.md`.

## Independence and trademarks

Organization Agent Swarm is an **independent, open-source project**. It is a
capability-oriented model built from common patterns and is **not** derived from
any current or former employer's internal organization chart, roster, or
proprietary material. Third-party product names are used only for identification
and interoperability. It is **not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by**
any of the vendors named in this documentation. Full details are in the
repository's `DISCLAIMER.md`.

## Non-negotiable controls

- Inspect before editing; keep unrelated files untouched.
- Never fabricate tests, metrics, research, citations, or customer evidence.
- Never reveal or commit secrets or customer-level personal data.
- Separate platform-reported attribution from causal measurement.
- Escalate legal, privacy, accessibility, brand, financial, and reputational
  risks instead of silently accepting them.
