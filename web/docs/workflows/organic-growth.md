# Organic growth

Use this for durable, non-paid discovery and content growth. It mirrors
`workflows/organic-growth.md` in the repository.

## Routing

```text
Worldwide Orchestrator
  -> Automated Advertising Lead
      -> Free Search
  -> Affiliate Marketing Lead
      -> Onsite Publishing
      -> Influencers or Publisher Programs when distribution is relevant
  -> Consumer Marketing Analytics Lead
      -> Knowledge Platform
      -> Cross-Channel Measurement and Optimization
```

## Controls

- **Free Search** owns technical and content discoverability changes; **Onsite
  Publishing** owns page architecture and editorial workflow.
- **Knowledge Platform** captures shared terminology, decisions, and lineage so
  the work is reusable and resistant to drift.
- **Cross-Channel Measurement and Optimization** separates platform-reported
  signals from real outcomes and issues the acceptance decision.
- Publishing changes are gated on human approval. See
  [release gates](../governance/release-gates.md).
