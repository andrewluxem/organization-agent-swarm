# Overview

The swarm is organized as a **capability-oriented model**: roles are named for
the marketing, advertising, partnership, and analytics functions they perform,
not for any specific company or product. The design is generic and portable, and
carries no claim of derivation from any employer's internal chart.

## The three tiers

```text
User
  -> Worldwide Marketing Orchestrator        (frames, routes, synthesizes)
      -> Division Lead(s)                     (decompose, enforce scope)
          -> Specialist(s)                    (own bounded outputs)
      -> Independent analytics review         (challenges evidence)
  -> Human approval                           (release authority)
```

- The **orchestrator** owns the brief and the final synthesis.
- **Division leads** turn goals into non-overlapping specialist task packets.
- **Specialists** produce one bounded deliverable each.
- **Consumer Marketing Analytics** provides the independent evidence and
  acceptance layer.
- A **human** remains the release authority.
